We use paper EVERY DAY, but, have you ever stopped to think about
what paper really is? Of course as children we are taught that paper
is made from trees, but this doesnt really give us a good explination
of what the makeup of paper is. Papermaking is art of, well... making paper. In order to make paper, we need to first understand what paper is. paper is made from cellulose fibers, usually derived from plants such as grains (and yes, wood). These fibers are processed into pulp using either heat, repeated pumelling, or chemical processes. they are screened into a flat sheet, and left to dry.
